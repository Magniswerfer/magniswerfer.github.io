# Hi everyone!
I'm Magnus, an interaction designer and reacher currently working as a PhD Fellow in the [Dept. of Computer Science at Aarhus University, Denmark](https://cs.au.dk). Here, I work on designing interactive technologies for teaching about AI and machine learning from a critical point of view acknowledging that these technologies have immense promises for social and economical progress, but are also currently used by companies to nudge us to spend our time and money with them. My goal is to contribute to research that empowers users of AI and machine learning (children, adolescents and adults alike) to be mindful of how and when we interact with them, and to retain (or regain) agency in doing so. I take a designerly and interventionist approach to this, by designing and prototyping functional tools that I employ in classrooms and by collaborating with municipalities, teachers and companies with interests similar to mine.

Apart from my current research, I am also interested in design research, more broadly, and in designing new interaction techniques such as shape-changing interfaces and human-robot interaction.

When I'm not working I love making things for myself, my family and my friends. I currently live in a large house on the countryside outside of Aarhus with my partner and 5 friends, and I love the commitment necessary to make such a living arrangement work, having a garden that provides vegetables for us, and the refreshing 30 minute bike ride each morning and afternoon. In fact, I am really into road cycling, and I am currently following the Tour de France very closely!

Please have a look around my website. I have gathered my projects in a [portfolio](/projects), my [publications](/publications) and my [resumé](/resume). For any further enquiries, feel free to reach out via my [contact page](/contact).
