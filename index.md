# Hi everyone!
I'm Magnus, but I suspect you knew that since you're visiting.

In a nutshell, I'm a researcher/designer currently working on my PhD Fellowship on designing learning tools for teaching secondary school students about machine learning and its (future) impacts.

When I'm not working I love making things for myself, my family and my friends. I'm currently obsessed with book binding and (vegan) leatherworking, but I also like all sorts of other creative endevours such as photography, 3D printing and electronics tinkering. As I work my way through this website, I'll upload some of my projects here, but in the mean time you can take a look at my [publications](/publications) as an appetizer.
